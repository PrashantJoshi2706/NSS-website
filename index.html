WHAT ARE WE


NSS NITC is the NIT Calicut chapter of National Service Scheme, working under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports Government of India. It was established in 1970 and since then, we have been actively instilling the importance of social responsibility in the minds of students. The NSS Chapter of NIT Calicut has four units, each guided by corresponding coordinators, with Dr. Raghu CV as the faculty in charge of NSS NITC. We have around 470 members who take part in community services in and around the campus. With the help of our volunteers, we have been able to spread the motto of “NOT ME BUT YOU” throughout the campus.



WHAT WE DO


NSS involves students in activities and tasks which thusly develops their skills and moral standards.NSS NITC has four units working under it.We organised several teaching programs including those for the underprivileged ,workshops and camps involving NITC student volunteers and talks by experts.We conducted cleaning drives in Calicut beach and subways in the locality,blood donation camps and a mini marathon.We arranged social initiative programs, 'Aavishkar' as a part of Tathva and 'Sneharagam' in connection with our cultural fest Ragam.NSS NIT Calicut has proven beneficial to the students and the society through the series of philanthropic efforts



OUR MISSION


NSS is a government program designed to inculcate the idea of welfare in students.Our sole aim is to motivate students to indulge in nation building activities through various events and projects,which are aimed towards the benefit of the people in and around NIT Calicut. We work on a diverse range of social issues including blood donation, teaching, environmental issues etc. We aim to stay true to the motto " Not Me, But You", reflects the essence of democratic living and upholds the need for selfless service.
